DMQL Project : Spring 2022

Project name : Digital Music store

Team Name : Quarks

Team Members : Shiva Kumar Reddy, Madhukar Reddy, Sai Sriram

Problem Statement :

Consumer welfare has long been recognized as a benefit of digitization, particularly in the form of online retailing. Greater consumer access to information has resulted in significant research into how the internet influences the types of items that generate most of the income. Digitization of the music consumption falls under the same bracket. Music enthusiasts may now effortlessly enjoy and benefit from a greater choice of products at a low cost thanks to the Digital Music Stores. The goal of this project is to identify and handle some of the key challenges that are faced by music companies whenever a decision is to be made that involves the history of the music consumption. With efficient data access queries and the luxury of having enormous amount of consumer data, this project helps the digital music stores to offer wide range of effective products based on consumer interests, geography etc. and to make smarter business decisions. Use of Database system over an excel files helps in dealing with larger data sets efficiently. A database server like PostgreSQL performs much better when compared to a software application like excel. SQL queries when compared to the excel formulas are more powerful and flexible. The use of Data Constraints to various types of information works better in SQL over excel as these constraints applies dynamically to any new entry. SQL is better in terms of storing and manipulating the data as SQL stores different kinds of information under meaningful tables which can be interconnected to obtain desired results as opposed to excel.
